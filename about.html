About Dreamotion

Dreamotion is a professional indoor playground manufacturer based in Guangzhou, China. With over 8 years of industry experience and a fully owned production facility, we specialize in customized design, high-quality production, and one-stop project support for clients around the world.

Where Our Products Are Used

Shopping malls, kindergartens, early education centers, hotels, and family entertainment centers. We’ve successfully exported to Vietnam, Singapore, Malaysia, Saudi Arabia, Nigeria and more.

Our Factory Strength

Unlike trading companies or resellers, Dreamotion owns a complete in-house factory covering steel frame welding, leather cutting, assembly, and ball pool testing zones. Every step — from design to delivery — is controlled by our team to ensure consistent quality and reliable lead times.

Beyond Manufacturing

More than a manufacturer, we are your project partner. We help entrepreneurs and business owners launch indoor playgrounds by offering layout design, site analysis, product planning, installation advice, and marketing support.
